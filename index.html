01 — The story

It started because
people needed groceries.

In April 2021, India was in lockdown and I was a computer science student with nothing but time. People in my hometown couldn't get essential goods. So I started delivering them.

That was DroliaFresh — a hyperlocal delivery operation, built out of a simple observation that things people needed were not reaching them.

When the lockdown lifted, the need it was built for disappeared. So we pivoted. DroliaFresh became a marketplace business focused on home cleaning supplies — the everyday products people restock without thinking, and rarely love.

Today we sell across Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit and Instamart, reaching more than 10,000 customers every month across India.

“Fresh, effective and honest.” Home cleaning is a category built on overpromises. We'd rather be the brand that just tells you the truth about what's in the bottle.
